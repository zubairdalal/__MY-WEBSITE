New Potato Fry

IMAGE AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION

Ingredients

  • 450g Small Potatoes
  • 3TbspOil
  • 1TspMustard Seeds
  • 1TspRed Chilli Powder
  • 1 1/2Tsp Ground Coriander Powder
  • 1 1/4Tsp Ground Tumeric Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt (Table or Pink Salt)
  • 3 Sprigs of Coriander, chopped
  • Lemon Juice (for taste)

Kitchen Materials

  • Hob (Gas or Electric)
  • Large Bowl
  • Saucepan with Lid (ideally a vent lid)
  • Wooden Spoon
  • Colander / Strainer for Rice
  • Container for Water

Prepration

  1. In the large bowl, soak the rice in water for 30 minutes
  2. Under luke warm water, by using your hands, a spoon or by shaking the Collander / Strainer, move the rice around until the water run clear (this removes starch)
  3. Tilt the Collander / Strainer at an angle to drain any water left
  4. In a container, add 1 1/2 cups of cold water

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a wok
  2. Add Mustard Seeds and Potatoes
  3. Stir on low heat until Potatoes are slightly brown
  4. Add Salt, Chilli Powder, Turmeric and Ground Coriander.
  5. Stir on low heat for 5-6 minutes
  6. Remove from heat
  7. Add coriander and lemon juice
  8. It's ready to SERVE!
