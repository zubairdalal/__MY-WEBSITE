Jeera Rice

IMAGE AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Rice
  • 3/4 Tbsp Oil (Sunflower or Olive Oil)
  • 1 Tsp Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
  • 3 whole Cloves
  • 2 Cardamom Pods
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 3/4 Tsp Salt (Table or Pink Salt)

Kitchen Materials

  • Hob (Gas or Electric)
  • Large Bowl
  • Saucepan with Lid (ideally a vent lid)
  • Wooden Spoon
  • Colander / Strainer for Rice
  • Container for Water

Prepration

  1. In the large bowl, soak the rice in water for 30 minutes
  2. Under luke warm water, by using your hands, a spoon or by shaking the Colander / Strainer, move the rice around until the water run clear (this removes starch)
  3. Tilt the Colander / Strainer at an angle to drain any water left
  4. In a container, add 1 1/2 cups of cold water

Steps

  1. Switch the Hob on a medium heat
  2. Add the Oil to the Saucepan, then place the Saucepan on the Hob
  3. Allow the Oil to heat up (around 1 minute)
  4. Add the Cumin Seeds, Cloves, Cardamom and Bay Leaf
  5. Allow the ingredients to heat up, until the smell becomes fragrance (around 1 minute)
  6. Add the rice in
  7. Turn the Hob to a High heat
  8. Stir the rice in with the ingredients for around 2 minutes
  9. Add the Cold Water
  10. Bring the Water to the boil
  11. WHen the Water is begins to bubble, turn the heat to the lowest and give the rice a stir
  12. Place the lid on and cook for 9 minutes
  13. After 9 minutes, turn the heat off
  14. Remove lid, and carefully stir the rice in by moving the rice from the bottom half off the lid to the top
  15. Place the lid back on and allow it to rest for another 5 minutes
  16. It's ready to SERVE!
